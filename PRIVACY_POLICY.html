We fully respect your privacy and collect zero personal data. ### Data We Collect **None.** The app does not collect any personal data whatsoever. Specifically: - ❌ No name, email, or phone number collection - ❌ No location tracking - ❌ No usage or browsing behavior tracking - ❌ No tracking cookies - ❌ No advertisements or data sharing with advertisers - ❌ No analytics tools (no Google Analytics, no Facebook SDK) ### Locally Stored Data The app stores the following data **only on your device** using localStorage: - Reading position (bookmark) to resume reading - Language, speed, and theme preferences - Personal word count and reading time This data is **never sent to any server** and only you can access it. You can delete it anytime by clearing the app's data. ### Visitor Counter The app uses [CounterAPI](https://counterapi.dev) solely to count total visits and collective reading time. This counter: - Stores only a single number (total visit count) - Does not store IP addresses or any identifying information - Cannot identify any individual user ### Quran Data The app retrieves Quran text from [api.alquran.cloud](https://alquran.cloud/api), a free open-source service. We do not control the privacy policy of this third-party service. ### Eye Tracking (Optional) The eye-tracking feature uses the device camera **locally only** to track eye movement. No images or camera data are ever sent to any server. Camera permission is only requested when you enable this feature, and you can decline it. ### Children The app is safe for all ages and does not collect any data from children or anyone else. ### Changes We may update this policy as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. ### Contact For any privacy inquiries, contact us at: a@eyepoke.com ---
